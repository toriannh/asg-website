The accountability layer

AI tools can generate pipelines, dashboards, and queries. They cannot sit in a room with your CFO and explain why the numbers don't match. They cannot be accountable when something breaks at 2 am before a board meeting.

Aquila exists where expertise, judgment, and ownership intersect — the layer that separates data you have from data you trust.
